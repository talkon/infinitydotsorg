InfinityDots MO

InfinityDots MO is a math contest simulating the International Mathematical Olympiad. We held the first iteration back in March 2017. As we received overwhemingly good feedback, we decided to make InfinityDots MO an annual event.

The next iteration is InfinityDots MO3, which will be held late March to early April 2019, with sign-ups starting early March. Stay tuned for more updates!
