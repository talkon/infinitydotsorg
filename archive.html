Test Archive

Special thanks to MarkBcc168 and jeneva for helping us with the archive, and also to recent Thailand IMO team leaders for providing us with official copies of the tests.

The vast majority of October Camp and TST problems are from respective IMO shortlists; we have opted not to include individual problem sources in our files, even when known, in order to simulate an actual exam setting.

Other Resources

Math Olympiad of the Storm →

Official site of the Thailand October and TST camps from 2015-16 onwards.

The MOOTS cloud contains official TST exams for 2016, 2017, and 2019, official 2016 and 2018 October Camp exams, and also a selection of handouts.

Mathcenter →

Its exams page contains some Thai MO (precursor to TMO) problems from 1989 to 2003.

Its webboard used to be popular with Thai math olympiad campers, and still contains various discussion threads from times past.

POSN Exams Page →

The official site for Thailand's national math and science olympiads. Contains the official files (in Thai) of most TMOs

The National Olympiads page also contains some official information about TMO.

Math Camp Wiki →

Contains some information about Thailand Mock IMOs up to 2017.

Kukkai.org →

Contains competitions hosted at Triam Udom Suksa School from 2003-2009.
